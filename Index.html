WELCOME EVERYONE; LET'S GET STARTED

Welcome Everyone The aim and objective of this group. The Core Vison of this group lies within our aim and objective which includes; supporting people build on their skills, learning new skills, apprenticeship support, personal development, and human capital empowerment. This group aims to hosts people from across all region with the singular purpose of helping hands, supporting people reach their goals, through mentorship, skill acquisition training, capital, and financial support where neccessary.

Why ASDF Matters

The Responsibility to provide Quality Education rest upon Us All As a non profit organisation, we rely on support both financially and in kind to accomplish our aims: Hence we seek public support to complete some of our ongoing projects and accomplish some of our missions. It is our desire to implement some of our projects in some schools and fix some challenges like classroom roofs, fence, toilets etc. before the children return to the classroom. We welcome volunteers and sponsors alike in partnering to put smiles on the faces of our children.
